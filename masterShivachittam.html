Master Shivachittam

Shivachittam, who is a Karma Sannyasi initiated by Swami Niranjananda Saraswati, learned yoga in his early childhood from Bihar School of yoga and has been practicing since then. After a dedicated practice for nearly three decades, he chooses to propel the wisdom of his guru.

In the years before dedicating himself fully to this prominent cause, Shivachittam gained education in Psychotherapy and Counselling, Business Administration, Journalism, and Mass Communication, and Sciences.

He has helped evolve life of many corporate employees and other working individuals to manage their work-life stress, transformed countless individuals suffering from illnesses like spinal disorders, mental health issues, Slipped disc issues, Sciatica, Back pain, Cervical spondylitis, etc. He has trained and transformed Famous Bollywood actresses, Public Figures, Sportsmen, Top-level corporate heads, and Business Magnets.

He has followers from all around the world and works all round the clock to transfigure the existence of humankind and rid them from their sufferings with help of yoga, meditation, and other alternative methods.

Master Shivachittam is a Naturopathist with a diploma in naturopath and yoga (NDDY) as well. He is a certified Yoga Teacher by the Quality Council of India (QCI) and the ministry of the AYUSH department, Government of India. His overall exposure and interests allow him to nicely blend the yogic practices with daily life. This offers his students a holistic and practical experience of yoga.
